HOW VENOM SYSTEMS WORKS


Venom security systems is a wholly owned Malawian company that provieds high quality and reliable electronic security systems. We have built our reputation around quality and service excellence by having direct links to selected companies of various electronic security technologies. The servive also extends to being in the unique position of accessing training, technical support and prices directly from the manufactures. Venim tech also specializes in the design, installation and maintenance of CCTV access control and intergrated systems within the commercial, industrial, educational and municipal sectors.
